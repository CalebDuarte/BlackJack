How to Play Blackjack

Get as close to 21 as possible without going over.

Number cards are worth their number.

J, Q, and K are worth 10.

Aces count as 11 unless that would make you bust, then they count as 1.

Hit gives you another card.

Stand ends your turn.

Double doubles your bet, gives one more card, then ends your turn.

All In adds all your chips into the current bet.

The dealer must draw until reaching at least 17.
